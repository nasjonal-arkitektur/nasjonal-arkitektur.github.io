ISO/IEC JTC 1/AG 8: Meta Reference Architecture and Reference Architecture for Systems Integration

Background

ISO JTC 1 is the standards development environment where experts come together to develop worldwide Information and Communication Technology (ICT) standards for business and consumer applications. Additionally, JTC 1 provides the standards approval environment for integrating diverse and complex ICT technologies. These standards rely upon the core infrastructure technologies developed by JTC 1 centers of expertise complemented by specifications developed in other organizations.

JTC 1 established AG8 on Meta Reference Architecture and Reference Architecture for Systems Integration in November 2018 with the following Terms of Reference:

  • Investigate and identify the current practices (including definition, concepts, processes, models, and templates) for development of Reference Architecture and Meta Reference Architecture in Systems Integration contexts that are relevant to JTC 1;

  • Develop a document that contains a definition, concepts, processes, models, and templates on Meta Reference Architecture for Systems Integration entities;

  • Provide recommendations for the actions for JTC 1 to successfully execute the Systems Integration efforts by utilizing Meta Reference Architecture;

AG8 standing documents

AG8 is producing three standing documents:

  1. standing document 1: Analysis of RA-related standards provides a comprehensive analysis of RArelated standards, lists issues and solutions identified during the Montreal workshop, and provides conclusions on the analysis

  2. standing document 2: Convergent Reference Architectures takes into account the content of standing document 1, as well as the results from the specific expert sessions during the Montreal workshop, and provides an analysis on the following topics:

    • the nature of reference architecture,

    • the scope and granularity of reference architectures,

    • integration issues of RAs,

    • convergence on commonalities,

    • convergence on vocabulary and notations,

    • convergence on integration, and

    • elements for a meta RA based on a proposed common notation;

  3. standing document 3: Roadmap for a Common RA stack takes into account the contents of standing documents 1 and 2, as well as the results from the specific expert sessions on roadmap during the Montreal workshop, and provides recommendations for a roadmap.

Other documents

As of 2020-03-05, AG8 has produced a total of 180 documents, numbered from N0001 to N0180, including standing documents, meeting minutes, plans, member lists, reports and various other documents.
